What is the Robert Hattersley Foundation?

The Robert Hattersley Foundation is a not-for-profit foundation active in the Tyne valley and Gateshead area. We are a registered charity and our goal is to educate and inform children, carers and teachers of the dangers of open waters. We are a local and majority family run and operated organisation. We have formed The Robert Hattersley Foundation to help raise awareness of the dangers open waters. We will visit primary/secondary schools to raise awareness about the dangers of open waters to help keep young children safe when they are near these dangerous waters. This is so no one else will lose a loved one when they enter the waters so no tragedies can occur around open waters. We also wish to support young people who suffer with mental health who have lost a loved one or a close friend through drowning.

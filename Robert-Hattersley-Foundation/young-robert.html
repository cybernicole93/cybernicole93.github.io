The Tragic tale of Robert Hattersley

Robert Hattersley had a bright future ahead of him. He had many friends, a successful school career and was an avid young sportsman. He brought joy to everyoneâ€™s life through his sense of humor and practical jokes, as well as his kind and loving heart. Robert Hattersley truly was a blessing on everyone who knew him.

One evening, Robert and his friends were playing in the river Tyne, near Crawcrook. He fell in and was unable to resist the strong current of the river. Despite the best attempts of the emergency services and his friends who were with him, they couldn't get Robert out. The children were left shook with grief, and his family and friends were devastated, but they transformed that grief into energy and effort: founding the Robert Hatterley Foundation as his legacy.
